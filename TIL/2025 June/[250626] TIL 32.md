137.
```sql
select *
from (
    select city
        ,   length(city) as city_length
    from STATION
    order by city_length, city asc
    limit 1
) as a
union all
(
    select city
        ,   length(city) as city_length
    from STATION
    order by city_length desc, city asc
    limit 1
)
```
